      In the terminal, a 5-minute poller matches a poem to your live
      conditions — Open-Meteo weather,
      time of day, season, and festival proximity — and hot-reloads Ghostty in
      place. The sliders above re-bake the same #defines the swap
      script injects (intensity and the mood / energy / density / glow feeling
      dials) and recompile — exactly how a real swap works. Text stays legible
      because scenes composite behind the glyph layer, here simulated
      with a fake terminal screenful.
    

    

      MIT licensed. The tab pauses itself when hidden — the same
      don't-burn-the-battery ethos as the terminal version.
